TOS

Payment TOS

  • Half of payment owed is due up front, last payment is to be made before commissioner receives the full unmarked file

  • Any significant changes made after completion is a $5 charge, some things may be unchangable and can be refused

  • Quotes are always free! just message me and ask if you arent sure the price of something

  • All prices are listed in USD (U.S Dollars) so be mindful of that!

  • I will only accept payment via paypal, venmo, or cashapp, I do NOT accept any digital checks

  • No refunds after payment has been sent, the commission can be canceled halfway through by the commissioner but the first payment will not be refunded

  • Extra fees will be stated and agreed upon before a commission is started

    • Rights

  • ALL artwork other than tattoos will be watermarked and/or signed, any removal (Photoshoping, cropping (not in the case of profile photos), etc of credit will result in a blacklist from any future commissions

  • Feel free to post my artwork wherever you want as long as credit is made!

  • Do not edit my work to the point it is unrecognisable, do not color any uncolored sketches

  • Any use of AI with my work will result in a complete blacklist from any future commissions or reposting of my art

  • I reserve the right to post any artwork I make onto my social medias and websites, if you want something delayed/hidden for personal reasons please let me know

  • Do not commission other artists to edit and complete my work

    • General TOS

  • You will receive updates through every step in the process and upon reasonable request. please mind my timezone is EST and other life factors Such as me being a full time student will affect how fast I can deliver update requests

  • Wait time will be discussed during the intitial planning stage, I on average take 1-2 weeks for character art, detailed paintings may take longer

  • Note that I am disabled and live with chronic issues, if my symptoms ever cause a delay this will be communicated to the commissioner, if I end up in a state where I am hospitalized/unable to finish, refunds will be issued as soon as possible

  • Harrasment, insulting etc to the artist will result in a decline of a commission/cancelation with no refund

  • If contact is cut mid commission and I cannot get to you, the commission will be considered finished and a refund will not be issued

  • ANY NSFW piece must only ever be posted in 18+ communities/not available to minors, if you are found to break this you will be blacklisted from commissioning me in the future

  • I may ask for proof of age before accepting NSFW commissions, this will be done by requestiong a photo of your ID with your face in it, any sensitive info can be blurred out!

  • I reserve the right to deny any commission for any reason
